Placement By Types

Indoor planting

“Indoor planting involves growing plants inside homes or buildings to improve air quality, reduce stress, and add natural beauty. It creates a healthier and more pleasant indoor environment.”

Outdoor plantion

“Outdoor planting involves growing trees, shrubs, and plants in open areas to create greener, healthier, and more sustainable environments. It supports biodiversity and improves overall surroundings.

Bathroom planting

“Bathroom planting adds a fresh, calming touch by using moisture-loving plants that thrive in humid, low-light conditions. It enhances air quality, softens hard surfaces, and brings a natural, spa-like feel to the space..

Room Planting

Room planting involves placing indoor plants in bedrooms or living rooms to enhance air quality, comfort, and visual appeal. It creates a more relaxing and healthy atmosphere.

Window Planting

Window planting involves placing plants on windowsills or near windows to maximize natural light. It brightens the space, improves air quality, and creates a cheerful, green view both inside and outside the room.

Kichan Planting

Kitchen planting brings life and freshness to the space by adding herbs or compact greenery. It improves air quality, adds natural color, lively atmosphere while keeping the kitchen functional and inviting..
